Matcha Latte

Description

Green Tea Flavored Latte based on the popular Japanese ceremonial tea (my personal favorite).

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup vanilla soy milk
  • 1 teaspoon green tea powder (matcha)
  • 1 teaspoon granular sucralose sweetener (such as SplendaÂ®), or to taste
  • ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Pour soy milk into a blender. Add matcha; blend until dissolved and the mixture is smooth, about 30 seconds. Add sucralose and blend until combined, about 10 seconds.
  2. Fill a glass with ice and pour in green tea mixture.
